WHAT WE DO

Nurturing little minds
that will build tomorrow, today.

At Rising India School of Excellence (RISE) Preschool, we are building the future of India. Starting with its youngest minds. As Goa's first truly international preschool, we offer an education that transcends the classroom. Many early learning centres don't offer children the chance to truly explore and discover. This often stifles their natural sense of wonder, curiosity and the joy of learning - the very foundation for future leaders and creators. What we do is create a world of Risers who are confident, curious and creative through our proprietary D.I.S.C.O.V.E.R.Y. Mind ModelⒸ which equips young children with the mindset and skills to become future leaders and innovators.

HOW WE DO IT

A bespoke curriculum.
For innovative learning and holistic growth.

Our cutting-edge curriculum blends modern teaching methods with state-of-the-art infrastructure. With a focus on holistic development, we move beyond rote learning to embrace discovery, creativity, critical thinking and character-building.

We rise beyond traditional learning.

At Rising India School of Excellence, we don't just teach - we let children discover. Our unique D.I.S.C.O.V.E.R.Y. Mind Model© fuels curiosity, encouraging kids to learn through experience and not just instruction.

Learning without limits.

Classrooms can't contain us. With 20 immersive experience zones, children move freely, exploring and learning through hands-on activities in an ever-evolving environment.

Building future-ready skills.

Knowledge is just the beginning. Our D.I.S.C.O.V.E.R.Y. curriculum develops essential skills and mindsets, shaping adaptable, creative, and resilient individuals prepared for the future.

Beyond traditional subjects.

We go beyond the basics. From STEM to performing arts to international languages, our programmes ignite imagination and foster global perspectives.

Excellence in every detail.

Excellence is our foundation. That’s why we partner with the best to ensure every aspect of our school meets the highest standards for your child's success.

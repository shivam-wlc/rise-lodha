WHERE THE FUTURE OF EDUCATION
AND THE FUTURE OF INDIA RISE

“Elevating education for a rising India to nurture thinkers, innovators and leaders who will shape the country’s ascent as a global powerhouse.”

Rising India School of Excellence is not just an institution; it is a calling, a dream and a movement dedicated to nation-building. This school represents the deep-seated desire of a team of pedigreed professionals and acclaimed educationists to contribute to the progress and development of our great nation, fostering future leaders who will take India to even greater heights.

In the 21st century - the age of Artificial Intelligence - India's trajectory is set on the path to global leadership. But for this vision to become a reality, we need thinkers, innovators and leaders who can make a difference. This requires education that goes beyond classrooms and textbooks. We need transformative education for a rising India. Education which modernises knowledge, and complements it with skills, character and meta-learning.

Through a progressive and dynamic curriculum that blends modern teaching methodologies, state-of-the-art infrastructure and dedicated faculty, we are committed to fostering the development of holistic well-rounded individuals who are prepared to thrive in a fast-changing world.

Payal Gaba

CEO & Founder

A successful business leader and educator, Payal Gaba has a degree in science and an MBA. She worked in leading organisations like Maruti and Samsung before founding BeyondSkool, an upskilling academy for kids. As the CEO of RISE School, she aims to revolutionise early childhood education by fostering creativity, curiosity and resilience in children.

Pritam Bisht

Education Strategist

Pritam Bisht has over 20 years of experience in management consulting, specialising in corporate strategy, business development and marketing. He wants to impact the education ecosystem with his expertise and drive for innovation. His forward-thinking approach has helped create something relevant for the fast-changing world.

Zeenat Bandukwala

Pedagogy & Learning Advisor

Zeenat started her career in research at Nielsen. She then joined Teach for India as a Fellow and later trained teachers, wrote curriculum and measured student impact. As co-founder and Chief Academic Officer at BeyondSkool, she created 21st century skills curricula. She is now pursuing a PhD to understand the core competencies of exceptional teachers.

Leena Pimpley

Curriculum Advisor & Specialist

An educationist with more than 30 years of experience in varied roles ranging from being a Principal for an IB World School to National Head of Academics for the Aga Khan Education Service to Programme Leader for the Cambridge International Professional Development course to School Quality Assessor and mentor to many international schools and non-government organisations, Dr. Leena holds a PhD from Mumbai University and is passionate about developing progressive and dynamic curricula relevant to our fast-changing world.

Dr. Shilpa Agrawal

Child Development Expert

Dr. Shilpa holds a Ph.D. in Child Development and has over 15 years of expertise in early childhood education. She is deeply passionate about creating a nurturing, stimulating and safe environment for children. With an extensive background in child and adolescent psychology, Dr. Shilpa has closely collaborated with children, parents and educators throughout her career. She understands the significance of fostering emotional, social and academic growth.
